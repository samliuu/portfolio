Typography 1

This was a 14 week long project from ARTG3350 Typography 2 course. The purpose of this class was to create a poster and a book to understand different aspects of typography. The main focus of the poster was to incorporate typography terms, typography definitions, and images. The objectives of the book project were to create a book to explore structures and hierarchies through typography.

Adobe InDesign
Adobe Photoshop

Book Project

The theme I chose for my book project was Studio Ghibli's films. I enjoy watching them and I thought it would be a fun to grab different elements in the films to dissect. I started off with photos of different scenes and text about themes, characters, and plot. Throughout the semester, I arranged the images and text so that the information flowed smoothly though each page.



Key Takeaways

  • Font size: can be used to emphasize certain aspects for viewers to focus on
  • Image: affects visual flow, adds contrast to the poster
  • Placement and arrangement of text: helps create a composition where images and text can compliment each other
