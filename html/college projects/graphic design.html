Graphic Design 1

This was a 7 week course that encouraged exploration of form and composition. I produced a poster of my topic of choice and utilized the principles of visual heiarchy to represent my chosen words and images.


Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Timeline

May 2020 - June 2020

Hiearchy 1 - composition must contain organic line-work + object photography

Hiearchy 2 - composition must contain organic line-work + object photography + letterform

Hiearchy 3 - composition must contain organic line-work + object photography + letterform + body-part/vegetable photography

Hiearchy 4 - line work + object photography + letterform body-part/vegetable + texture

Hiearchy 5 - line work + object photography + letterform, body-part/vegetable + texture + word

Heiarchy 6 - take your 2 photographs, create a double exposure and recompose the existing 4 compositions replacing the 2 photos with the double exposure

Hiearchy 7 - you now have 4 compositions, each with 6 elements, each element bringing it's own graphic character and place in the hierarchy sequence
