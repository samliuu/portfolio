I created 24 compositions that work together as a series while incorporating each of the 24 typefaces used in your logotypes. This allowed me to explore type as illustration.






I was introduced to 24 typefaces and I learned to recognize the difference between what is successful and unsuccessful in the architecture of character design while sharpening my technical skills. I created a mark by combining two letter forms from the same font family and the design of the mark has to show an understanding between strokes and intersections. These logotypes are displayed in the logotype book.
