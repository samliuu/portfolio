Takeaways

Throughout my PC building journey, I gained a deeper understanding of how computers function, carefully selected parts that suited my needs, and learned the process of assembling the hardware. One crucial takeaway from the assembly experience is the importance of not rushing through the steps. To ensure everything is done correctly, it is essential to thoroughly read the manual multiple times or watch several YouTube tutorials to familiarize yourself with the detailed, step-by-step process (such as identifying which wire connects to which port).
