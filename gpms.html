Grant Partner Management Spreadsheet



Overview

The Grant Partner Management Spreadsheet houses all google spreadsheets that are used during the season and tracks data from different organzations for auditing and monitoring purposes.

The purpose of this project was to have one spreadsheet that links to the different spreadsheets that are typicall utilized intead of scrolling through a multiple folders.

Partner Hub

The previous design for the AHM tab (now named Partner Hub) only had the youth and youth leader allocation information, which were derivived from the participant roster.


The Partner Hub now is a combination of the Youth Data and Documentation Audit tab and the AHM tab. This makes it easier for specialists to access all of the information they need to audit in one space instead of needing to go through separate spreadsheets and folders. Some new additions to the Partner Hub are the status and the links to the applicant tracking data and youth participant roster. The payroll report links were also added.
